Please note the following:

* Catering: Exact numbers (with the Male & Female breakdown) must be communicated to Camp Eden Staff no later than 4 days before the camp. It is the responsibility of the camp organizers to report any increase / decrease in the number of campers booked for – to ensure adequate catering and avoid the purchase of excess perishable foods. If Camp Eden staff are not notified in time then meals will still be charged for at R38.00 per meal / per person to cover our costs.

* Payments: DEPOSIT - To secure your booking a deposit must be paid as soon as possible but at least within 2 weeks from when you made the booking. We cannot hold any bookings without the deposit having been paid. This must be paid directly into the Camp Eden bank account. Please use your ‘Group’s Name’ PLUS ‘Camp Eden’ as a reference for all transactions. Should the deposit not be paid within that time, we reserve the right to cancel your booking. - 100% of the deposit is non-refundable should the camp be cancelled 1 month or less before the camp. - 50% is refundable should the camp be cancelled within 3 months of the camp’s date. - 90% is refundable should the camp be cancelled over 3 months before the camp’s date. - Amounts to be deposited are as follows: R500 for the Bush Camp or R2000 for the Main campsite (this may be more for larger or longer camps).

* Payments: BALANCE - PLEASE NOTE THAT FOR SECURITY REASONS WE DO NOT ACCEPT CAMP PAYMENTS MADE IN CASH ON THE PREMISES. Please make the balance of payment via one of the following methods: Cheque, EFT (2 days prior), Cash Deposit (2 days prior).
